Important Documents for Admission

* Students are required to provide ORIGINAL + ATTESTED XEROX COPIES of all the documents mentioned below

* The list given below is minimum requirement for admission. Colleges having Linguistic,Religious Minority also require additional documents.

1) S.S.C. Marksheet

2) H.S.C. Marksheet

3) CET / AIEEE Scorecard

4) College Leaving Certificate

5) Domicile Certificate

6) Nationality Certificate ( If not mentioned 'Indian' in College Leaving Certificate or Birth Certificate)

7) Caste Certificate & Caste Validity Certificate ( If applying seats under Caste Quota )

8) Non-CreamyLayer Certificate ( If applying seats under Caste Quota, except SC/ST students)

9) GAP Certificate ( In case of gap in Education )

10) Migration Certificate (in case of candidate passing qualifying exam from any other Board other than Maharashtra State Board)
