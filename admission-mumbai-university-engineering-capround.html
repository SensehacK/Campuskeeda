Admission Procedure

Centralized Admission Process (CAP)

Student seeking admission in ENGINEERING in Colleges Affiliated to Mumbai University have to apply for CAP rounds conducted by DIRECTORATE OF TECHNICAL EDUCATION (DTE), Maharashtra State.

Procedure to Apply

1) Fill an online application form on DTE website (http://www.dte.org.in)
2) Take a printout of the filled application, please note your application ID and Password with which you registered on DTE website.
3) Get your documents verified at the nearest ARC (Application Form Receipt Center). ARC officer will verify your details and confirm your application . Collect acknowledgement receipt (stamped printout of the form)

List of all ARC in Maharashtra State.
Documents required during Verification at ARC.

4) After verification of documents DTE prepares a MERIT List of all the applicants. In case of error in personal details contact ARC and get it corrected .Wait for the display of Final Merit List on the DTE website.
5) You will be allotted a MERIT RANK according to your MHT-CET/AIEEE score on the DTE website.
Take a printout of your MERIT RANK. You will require this during CAP round at the ARC.
6) Mean while you can research on colleges, cutoffs, tuition fees, branches, etc. before start of CAP round-1.
You can visit :- List of Engineering Colleges for the same.
7) Apply for CAP rounds on the Scheduled dates.
8) There are in total four CAP rounds (including Counseling round).
CAP round - 1
9) You have to fill an online option form for the CAP round-I.All students are eligible for CAP round - I. In the option form you have to mention the colleges and branches of your choice.
10) You can fill a maximum of 20 options in CAP round – I.
11) Visit the same ARC center again & Submit your option form along with cash payment to be paid as CAP Round Processing Fee.Always carry the Stamped printout of the form (acknowledgement receipt) you get from the ARC during verification.

Important Note ( CAP round – 1)

Be careful while filling the option form because ,
If you are allotted a seat from the first 9 (1 - 9) choices you have submitted in option form then,
- You have to, COMPULSORILY take admission in that particular college and branch and
- You are not eligible for further CAP rounds
- You can however, choose not to take admission if you are allotted a seat from option 10-20 & you are not    happy with the allotted college , you will still be eligible for second CAP round.

    CAP round - 2
12) For CAP round – II repeat the same procedure of filling the option form online and submitting at the ARC like CAP round – I.
13) But You have to fill maximum of 30 choices in CAP round – II.

Important Note ( CAP round – 2)

Be careful while filling the option form because ,
If you are allotted a seat from first 15 (1-15) option then,
- You have to, COMPULSORILY take admission in that particular college and branch and
- You are not eligible for CAP round- III.
- You can now reject the options 16 to 30 if you are not happy with the seat allottment and will be eligible for third CAP round.

    CAP round - 3
14) For CAP round – III repeat the same procedure of filling the option form online and submitting at the ARC like previous CAP rounds
15) But You have to fill 45 choices in CAP round – III.

Important Note ( CAP round – 3)

- You are allotted a seat from the 45 option and you have to COMPULSORILY take admission in that college. - You are not eligible for further round (counseling).

    Counseling round
16) If you don’t get a seat allotment in third CAP round you have to report at Government College of Engineering, Aurangabad for personal Counselling session.

Important Note ( Counseling round )

- Students who didn't get seat allotment in the previous three CAP rounds are eligible for Counseling round.
