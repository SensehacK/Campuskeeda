About CampusKeeda

“Engineering is Hell” – that’s what you will get to hear from any engineering student. But engineering in Mumbai can be worse.

We don’t intend to scare you but that’s what our personal experience has been. Finding the right college after your CET/AIEEE results are out can be a really tedious job. It is the most important decision of your life because if you land in a wrong college, you could end up ruining your career. We have collected information about all the colleges in Mumbai University from the most reliable sources and is ready for your reference.

Once into the HELL, We mean Engineering College, the next big thing to do is to find the right syllabus. You would be amused to know that all books have their own set of chapters and even the professors are confused about the correct syllabus. We have the latest syllabus of all the branches for your use.

Engineering is vast, tough and believe me it really SUCKS!!!!

The best possible way to prepare for your semester exams is to solve past question papers because almost 40% of the questions keep on repeating every year. We have a collection of latest question papers in PDF format.
We hope our efforts prove to be a relief for the engineering students.

We wish you a successful career, Enjoy Engineering !!!
